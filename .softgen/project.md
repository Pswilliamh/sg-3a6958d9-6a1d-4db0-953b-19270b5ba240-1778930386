# Digital Chisel Gallery: Asian Culture Prints

## Vision
A premium, mobile-first Digital Sanctuary & Ambient Wallpaper application for curating Indonesian wood-relief prints. Serves collectors and contemplatives seeking a daily ritual anchor — art as meditation, not decoration. Operated by Digital Chisel Gallery (Kyoto temple workshop) in partnership with J.A.Y. Trade School.

## Design
**Palette** (near-monochrome restraint):
- `--background: 60 14% 96% (soft beige #F5F5DC)` — warm neutral ground
- `--foreground: 0 0% 10% (deep black #1A1A1A)` — text, structure
- `--muted: 210 14% 50% (slate gray #708090)` — secondary elements
- `--accent: 60 14% 96% (beige)` — minimal departure
- `--primary: 0 0% 10% (black)` — buttons, emphasis
- `--card: 0 0% 100% (white)` — overlays, cards

**Typography**: Noto Serif JP (headings, 400/600) + Noto Sans JP (body, 300/400) — cultural alignment, temple-grade restraint

**Style**: Wabi-sabi minimalism. Extreme negative space, Ken Burns motion (60s cycle) on all imagery, 3s cross-dissolve transitions, zero chrome unless load-bearing. Typography whisper-quiet. Long-press hides all UI.

## Features
1. **The Daily Anchor** (Tab 1) — Full-screen wood-relief art + wisdom quote overlay + 1min piano loop. Randomized daily quote. One-click wallpaper apply. Digital Chisel logo subtle upper corner.
2. **The Soundscape Gallery** (Tab 2) — Masonry grid of library images. Gapless audio loop with volume fader. Ken Burns synced to piano tempo.
3. **The J.A.Y. Embassy** (Tab 3) — Mission page: 7-Year Residency, Digital Chisel Technique, J.A.Y. Trade School. Hero placement of J.A.Y. logo. Support CTA. Social sharing (art + quote → branded vertical image). Link to kingdom-embassy.org.
4. **Core Infrastructure** — Wisdom quotes JSON, audio system (gapless loop), Ken Burns animation system, social export.
